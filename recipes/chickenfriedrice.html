Chicken Fried Rice

Description

Chicken friend rice is a simple and relatively healthy way to enjoy your leftovers. Feel free to substitute the frozen veggies with any leftover vegetables you might have laying around the house. You can also feel free to substitute the chicken breast with any leftover meats you might have.

Ingredients

  • 25oz chicken breast
  • 1 tblspn garlic powder
  • 2 tblspn soy sauce
  • 1 tblspn avocado oil
  • 1 tblspn sriracha
  • 300G mixed frozen veggies of choice
  • 200G egg whites
  • 3 eggs
  • 700G day-old cooked rice (300G dry weight)
  • 3 tblspn soy sauce
  • 3 tblspn sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Slice the chicken breast thin and dice into cubes. Then marinate in a bowl with the garlic powder, soy sauce, avocado oil, and sriracha
  2. Whisk together the egg whites and 3 eggs into a bowl until they are fully combined
  3. Cook the chicken in a pan on high heat in two or three batches for about 2-3 minutes until fully cooked through
  4. Once chicken is cooked through, remove the chicken and place mixed veggies in the pan until fully softened. Once the veggies are done, push them to the side of the pan and pour in the egg mixture
  5. Cook the eggs until they are scrambled, then mix all the ingredients together.
  6. Pour over the rice, top off with soy sauce and sesame oil, and mix well.
  7. Mix in the cooked chicken and enjoy
