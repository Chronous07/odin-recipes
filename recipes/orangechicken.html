Orange Chicken

Description

This is a lower calorie, no sugar added, orange chicken recipe to satisfy your chinese food cravings while still sticking to your diet! This recipe makes 5 servings so its perfect to feed the whole family, or save some leftovers for later

Ingredients

  • 32oz skinless chicken thighs
  • 5 tablespoons corn starch
  • 1/2 tspn onion powder
  • 1/2 tspn garlic powder
  • 1/2 tspn ginger powder
  • 1/2 tspn salt
  • 1 tspn black pepper
  • 280G basmati rice (uncooked weight)
Sauce
  • 3/4 cup orange juice
  • 2 tblspn avocado oil
  • 3 tblspn soy sauce
  • 1.5 tblspn rice vinegar
  • 2 tblspn zero calorie brown sugar
  • ~1/2 tblspn grated ginger
  • 3 cloves fresh minces garlic
  • 1/4 tspn black pepper
  • 1 tblspn sugar free orange marmalade
  • 1.5 tblspn corn starch

Instructions

  1. Mix together the corn starch, onion powder, garlic powder, ginger powder, salt, and black pepper to create a coating for the chicken
  2. Dice the chicken thighs into small cubes and coat with the mixture from the previous step until completely coated
  3. Lightly spray the chicken with cooking oil and airfry at 400F for 15 minutes or until fully cooked
  4. Cook rice following the packaging instructions. I recommend using a rice cooker if you have that option
  5. Place all the sauce ingredients in a pan over medium head until it begins to simmer. Continue simmering utnil its slightly thickened, but still fluid enough to move around the pan
  6. Mix the cooked chicken into the pan, toss to coat, and serve over the cooked rice
