Pizza Bagel Bites

Description

Enjoy a healthier version of your favorite pizza with these everything "bagel" pizza bites! In addition to being tasty and customizable to your own preferences these pizzas are also macro and calorie friendly.

Substitute standard english muffins for a low carb and calorie option to make them even healthier.

Ingredients

  • 10 English muffins
  • 400G Marinara Sauce
  • 50G Avocado oil
  • 33G Parmigiano reggiano
  • 60G Chopped hot peppers
  • 200G 2% Mozzarella
  • 60 Slices turkey pepperoni
  • 33G Hot honey
  • Oregano and Basil (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 450F
  2. Split and lightly spray each half of the english muffins with oil spray
  3. Place english muffins on baking sheet, and bake in the oven for approximately 5 minutes (or until crispy)
  4. Once english muffins are done, remove them from the over and add marinara sauce, chopped peppers, shredded mozzarella cheese, and pepperoni slices to each half
  5. Drizzle some hot honey over the top of each muffin half and place them back in the oven for another 5 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly
  6. Remove english muffins from the oven and top them with freshly grated parmesan cheese, and oregano, and basil
  7. Each "Serving" is 4 pizzas, seperate the extra food into containers of your choice and freeze to reheat later
